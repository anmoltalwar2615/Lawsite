Our Advice on 1st-Time Purchaser of Real Property

  • 1). Purchase Fund Shall Be Available

    The purchaser shall ensure the fund is in the bank account before signing the contract.

  • 2). Home Inspection

    It is recommended that the purchaser have a home engineer inspect the property before contract signing.

  • 3). Contract Execution

    The seller’s attorney sends the contract draft. The purchaser’s attorney reviews and sends it back for execution.

  • 4). Closing

    Once all documents are clear and approved, parties proceed to schedule the closing with final checks.
